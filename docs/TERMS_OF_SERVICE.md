## 2. Service Description

### 2.1 Overview

EFVM360 is an enterprise software platform designed to facilitate digital shift handover processes for railway operations. The Service provides tools for structured handover documentation, operational continuity tracking, incident logging, analytics, and reporting within railway operating environments.

### 2.2 Core Functionality

The Service includes, but is not limited to, the following capabilities:

- **Shift Handover Management:** Structured digital forms for recording and transmitting operational status between shifts, including equipment condition, pending tasks, safety observations, and anomaly reports.
- **Operational Dashboard:** Real-time and historical views of operational metrics, shift logs, and key performance indicators.
- **Analytics and Reporting:** Trend analysis, heatmaps, configurable reports, and PDF export functionality for operational data.
- **Offline Capability:** Progressive Web Application (PWA) architecture enabling data capture in low-connectivity or offline environments, with automatic synchronization upon reconnection.
- **Integration APIs:** Webhook-based integrations and data export endpoints for interoperability with third-party enterprise systems.
- **Audit Trail:** Immutable logging of all data entries, modifications, and user actions for compliance and traceability purposes.

### 2.3 Service Limitations

The Customer acknowledges and agrees that:

(a) **Not a Safety System.** EFVM360 is an information management and operational communication tool. It is NOT a safety-critical control system, signaling system, train protection system, or safety interlock. The Service does not directly control, monitor, or override any physical railway infrastructure, rolling stock, or safety equipment.

(b) **No Guarantee of Completeness.** While the Service is designed to support thorough and structured shift handovers, it does not guarantee the completeness, accuracy, or timeliness of information entered by Users. The quality of handover data depends on User input.

(c) **Connectivity Dependence.** Certain features, including real-time collaboration, webhook delivery, and data synchronization, require active network connectivity. Offline functionality is limited to data capture and local storage; synchronization occurs only when connectivity is restored.

(d) **Third-Party Dependencies.** The Service may rely on third-party cloud infrastructure, communication services, and software libraries. EFVM360 is not responsible for outages, vulnerabilities, or limitations attributable to third-party providers, though we will exercise commercially reasonable efforts to select and manage reliable providers.

(e) **Browser and Device Compatibility.** The Service is optimized for modern web browsers and supported device configurations as documented in our technical specifications. Performance on unsupported configurations is not guaranteed.
