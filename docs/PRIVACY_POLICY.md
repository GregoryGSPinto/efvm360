# Privacy Policy Status

This repository is not a hosted public service by default. There is no operator-specific privacy policy embedded in the codebase that should be presented as binding for a live customer deployment.

## What Can Be Said Safely

- Running the repository locally may store demo and operational data in browser storage and, if configured, in MySQL.
- The backend includes LGPD-oriented export and anonymization endpoints.
- Optional Azure integrations may process secrets or telemetry when explicitly configured by the operator.

## What Should Not Be Said

- Do not name a real company as controller or processor.
- Do not publish mailing addresses, DPO contacts, or international transfer clauses unless they are legally reviewed for the actual operator.
